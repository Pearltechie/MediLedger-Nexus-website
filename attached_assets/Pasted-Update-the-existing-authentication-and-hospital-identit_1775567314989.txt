- The Hedera account memo on HashScan should be Reddington Hospital

.setAccountMemo(hospitalName)

After successful signup, save the hospital name together with the hospital identity record.

Store it in the same persistent storage already being used for authentication/session management.

Suggested fields:

{
  hospitalName,
  accountId,
  did,
  walletAddress,
  createdAt
}

For hospitals that have already signed up before:

- Fetch their previously saved hospital name
- Do NOT show the hospital-name input form again
- Do NOT create a new Hedera account
- Do NOT generate another DID

Instead, restore their existing session and take them directly to the dashboard.

This is very important to prevent duplicate hospital identities.

Example:

or

Welcome to MediLedger Nexus, Reddington Hospital
